Our Story

2012
Ph.D Joris Dormans submits his research “Engineering emergence: applied theory for game design”. Ernest Adams and Joris Dormans publish “Game Mechanics: Advanced Game Mechanics”
2013
Joris Dormans and Ernest Adams present Machinations at GDCEurope during their talk “Machinations: A New Way to Design Game Mechanics”
2015
As a game economy designer at a successful studio in Denmark, Mihai Gheza starts using Machinations investigating how to base game design decisions on science, not opinions
While at GameAnalytics, Dana Gheza starts working on a series of in-depth cross-game data reports. Talking about the insights and industry challenges, Mihai shows Machinations to Dana. Bewildered by the potential, Mihai and Dana ask Joris about his future plans for Machinations
As a keynote speaker at Games Business, Mihai presents a Machinations diagram and witnesses the entire room being lit up by phone flashes in response
Mihai and Dana talk to Radu Craciun about how Machinations can dispel uncertainty in a hit-driven industry. Radu is fascinated and immediately sees the applicability to other industries. Inspired by the potential and audience reception, Mihai, Dana and Radu talk to Joris and team up to advance Machinations and port the Flash version into a browser-base collaborative platform
2016
Alin Moldovan and his team of kick-ass devs are sold into the vision and join ranks
2018
Fit4Start Accelerator grants them the first funding
Machinations.io launches in closed Beta — 700 users
2019
Seedcamp leads a pre-seed round
Machinations in Open Beta — 2000 users
2020
Machinations Live Collaborative Editing — 12000 users
Machinations launches it's Unity3D plugin — real-time 2-way sync between Machinations diagrams and the Unity build
GapMinder leads seed round
2021
Machinations launches a series of Live Webinars
The Machinations Community goes live — 23000 users
2022
HIRO Capital leads Series A round
Game Economy Health Monitoring Service
Machinations is nominated for Best Tech Provider at Develop:Star Awards, Brighton
Team-up with Prof. Edward Castronova, the inventor of the virtual economics field, to define ”The Machinations Manifesto For Building Sustainable Game Economies – The Design Pillars”
Custom Variables & Live Data Ingestion
2023
10 yrs anniversary GDC Talk: Building Sustainable Game Economies: The Three Design Pillars
