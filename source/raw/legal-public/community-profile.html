FAQ

What are Machinations Events?

Machinations Events represent state changes or formula computations that happen during simulations — whether you’re using Step Play, Interactive Play, Predict, or the Balancer. Every time your diagram updates (e.g., nodes/connections get activated, tokens move, formulas recalculate), it produces and consumes Events. The total number of Events consumed depends on: Diagram complexity (more nodes = more Events per step); Number of simulation steps, and Number of playthroughs (especially in Predict or Balancer). In short: the more your diagram “does,” the more Events it uses. Also, Events are consumed based on the number of AI tokens used by the AI Builder when responding to prompts.

What happens if I exceed my Monthly Events allowance?

You have options: wait for your next monthly refill; top-up your account with extra Events or upgrade your plan to get a higher monthly Event allocation. You won't be blocked - just choose the option that works best for you.

Does the AI Builder consume Machinations Events?

Yes — Based on the length of your request, the number and the size of the uploaded files and the complexity of the diagram context, the AI builder consumes Machinations Events when responses are generated.

Can I change my Monthly Events allowance?

Presently, you can do that by switching to a higher tier plan that includes a larger allocation.

How Events are used across Team Members?

To ensure that each team member can get the full value of a shared project, each new seat purchased includes a new batch of Machinations Events. For example for a team with 3 members, the number of monthly events will be tripled!

Do unused Monthly Evens roll over?

No — Monthly Events expire on a monthly basis (for both monthly and annual plans) and reset. Use them before they refresh.

Do unused Top-up Events roll over?

Yes — Top-up Events never expire. Once purchased, they stay in your account until used.

If I have both Monthly and Top-up Events, which are used first?

Your Monthly Events are always used first. Top-ups act as a safety net and only kick in when your monthly balance runs out.

Do you offer volume discounts for Top-up Events?

Yes. The larger the bundle you're, the lower the cost per Event.

Do plan tiers affect Top-up pricing?

Yes. Starter users get a 20% discount, while Pro plan users get a 50% discount on all Top-Up bundles.

Can I invite someone on a free plan to collaborate on a private diagram?

Yes, but they’ll only have view-only access until they switch to a paid plan. Editing private diagrams requires a Starter or Pro subscription.

What happens to my private diagrams if I downgrade to the Community plan?

They remain in your account, but you won’t be able to edit or simulate them. To continue working on them, either make them public or upgrade again.

Are Community plan diagrams visible to everyone?

Yes — all diagrams created in the Community plan are public by default and can be viewed, played, and forked by others.

What’s the difference between team folders and team management?

Team folders enable shared storage, visibility, and access control for all diagrams within a team. Team management allows you to manage roles, billing, and seat allocation for your organization.

What’s the difference between Team folder and shared private diagrams?

The Team Folder (Pro only) is a shared space that automatically shows diagrams shared with your entire team. Shared private diagrams are individual diagrams you’ve shared with another user on a paid plan — they’re visible and editable to invited users only.

What does “Simulation Accuracy” mean?

When you use Predict or Balancer, you’re running many simulations to estimate likely outcomes. Higher accuracy = more playthroughs = more reliable results. This is based on the Monte Carlo method, where larger sample sizes reduce randomness and improve predictions.
